# Gravitile Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2 July 2026

Gravitile is built to be private by design.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Gravitile collects no personal data, no analytics, no
identifiers, and no usage information. The app contains no advertising and
no third-party SDKs, and the app's own code makes no network requests.

## What stays on your device

Your game progress — scores, streaks, settings, and saved games — is stored
only on your device (and in your device backups, which you control). Deleting
the app deletes this data.

## Apple services

If you choose to use these optional Apple services, Apple processes that data
under [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/):

- **Game Center** — signing in lets Apple show your scores on leaderboards.
- **In-App Purchases** — payments are handled entirely by Apple. We never see
  your payment details.

Both are optional; the game is fully playable without them.

## Children

Gravitile is rated 4+ and collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will ship with the app
update that changes it, with the effective date above revised.
